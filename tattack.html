About this document

I took the time to write some of the specifications for an old SNES game named Tetris Attack (Panel De Pon in Japan). CMAC and I will be working on a Tetris Attack clone. I use the term "clone" because the mechanics will follow closely to the original game, but will not be the same. The final product might have mechanics not present in the original game, and the differences will hopefully create a more enriching experience.

Our objective is to improve our proficiency in problem solving, game design, game developing, programming, and become familiar with some common frameworks used in the industry.
